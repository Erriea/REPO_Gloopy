# AI Ethics Statement

This project used AI as a development assistance tool, not as the sole source of authorship or creative direction.

AI was used in the following ways:
- concept refinement and structuring of design ideas
- writing and revising gameplay code
- helping build and debug gameplay systems in Unity
- creating and adjusting simple custom shaders
- assisting with project documentation

The original game idea, overall creative direction, design decisions, implementation oversight, and final selection of features remained under the developer's control throughout the project.

All art and 3D assets used in the project were either created by the developer or sourced separately from online resources. All audio assets were likewise created by the developer or sourced separately online. AI was not used to generate the final art, 3D assets, or audio content used in the game.

AI-generated output was reviewed, tested, and iterated on by the developer before being accepted into the final project. The developer remained responsible for all final content, technical integration, and ethical use of third-party materials.

This statement is included to provide transparent disclosure of how AI contributed to the project.
